ACEing Caltech

Homepage for the Pasadena branch of ACEing Autism, located on the Caltech campus. Whether you're a parent interested in signing up your child for our program or someone looking to start their own branch, we have information for you!

Recent Updates

June 12th, 2024: Our spring 2024 session is complete. Thanks to everyone who came, we had a blast! Keep an eye out for information about the Fall 2024 Session coming out in September.

Jan 31st, 2023: ACEing Autism was featured on the Dr. Phil show. Our program directors, Jen Yu and Kyle McCandless, were invited to help out with the clinic and some of the students from our clinics at Caltech participated as well! Watch the episode here.

Aug 28th, 2022: After a 3-year absence, ACEing Autism held their annual clinic at the U.S. Open Tennis Championships. Program Director Jen Yu was invited to teach at the clinic. Watch the FOX5 New York coverage here.
