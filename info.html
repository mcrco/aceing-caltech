Spring 2025 Clinic

Our Fall 2025 clinic will take place on Sundays, 3pm - 4pm on 10/12, 10/19, 10/26, 11/2, 11/9, and 11/16 for a total of 6 sessions.

All of our spring clinics will take place at the Caltech Athenaeum on 551 S. Hill Ave. Please Park on Hill Avenue, and go up the stairs next to the tennis courts to find us. Below, we have pinned the exact location of our check-in desk. We should be easy to find on the days of the clinic!

We will be providing the following:

  • Racquets (but you can bring your own if you'd like)
  • Nets, balls, and other equipment
  • Name badges
  • (1st session) T-shirts for new participants/volunteers
  • Light snacks

Please bring a filled water bottle!
